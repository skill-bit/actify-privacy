Actify – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

1. Personal Data Collection

Actify does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App works entirely offline and no information is transmitted to external servers.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores minimal settings (sound, vibration, tutorial completion, favorites, and timer duration) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by deleting the App’s storage.

3. No Third-Party Services

Actify does not use analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, social media integrations, or cloud services.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is safe for children because it collects no personal data. Parental supervision is recommended when children use mobile devices.

5. Security

All game functionality occurs locally on the device. No remote servers or personal data are involved.

6. Internet Access

The App does not require internet access and does not send or receive any data over a network.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features. The “Last Updated” date will be updated accordingly.
