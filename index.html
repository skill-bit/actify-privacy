# Privacy Policy for Actify **Effective Date**: 2025-12-02 **Last Updated**: 2025-12-02 --- ## Introduction Thank you for using Actify ("we," "our," or "the app"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application. **Actify is committed to user privacy.** We collect minimal data, and you have full control over crash reporting through in-app settings. --- ## Information We Collect ### 1. Crash and Diagnostic Data (Optional) **What we collect:** - Device information (device model, operating system version) - App version and build type - Crash stack traces and error logs - Game state data at the time of crash (deck being played, score, timer settings) **How we collect it:** We use **Firebase Crashlytics**, a crash reporting service provided by Google LLC, to collect crash and diagnostic data. **User control:** - Crash reporting is **enabled by default** but can be **disabled at any time** in the app's Settings screen - No data is collected if you opt out - Toggle: Settings → Crash Reports → OFF **Purpose:** Crash data helps us identify and fix bugs, improve app stability, and enhance user experience. **What we DON'T collect:** - No personally identifiable information (PII) - No names, email addresses, or phone numbers - No precise location data - No user-generated content (custom decks are stored locally only) --- ## How We Use Information We use crash and diagnostic data solely to: - Identify and fix crashes and bugs - Improve app performance and stability - Understand how the app is used to prioritize feature improvements **We do NOT:** - Sell your data to third parties - Use data for advertising or marketing purposes - Share data with anyone except our service providers (Google Firebase) --- ## Data Storage and Security ### Firebase Crashlytics Crash data is processed and stored by **Google LLC** under their Firebase service. **Google's Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy **Data Retention:** - Crash reports are retained for **90 days** by Firebase Crashlytics - After 90 days, crash data is automatically deleted **Security:** Google Firebase implements industry-standard security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. --- ## Your Privacy Rights ### Right to Opt-Out You can disable crash reporting at any time: 1. Open Actify 2. Go to **Settings** 3. Toggle **Crash Reports** to **OFF** Once disabled, no crash data will be collected or sent. ### Right to Data Deletion Since we don't collect personally identifiable information, there is no user-specific data to delete. Crash reports are anonymized and automatically deleted after 90 days. If you have concerns about your data, contact us at: **[INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS]** ### GDPR Compliance (EU Users) If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR: - **Right to access**: Request information about data we process - **Right to erasure**: Request deletion of your data (note: crash data is anonymized) - **Right to object**: Opt out of crash reporting in Settings ### CCPA Compliance (California Users) California residents have the right to: - Know what data is collected (see "Information We Collect" above) - Opt out of data collection (disable crash reporting in Settings) - Request deletion of data (crash data is anonymized and auto-deleted after 90 days) --- ## Third-Party Services ### Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) **Service**: Crash reporting and diagnostics **Data Collected**: Device info, app version, crash logs, game state **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy **Data Processing:** Google processes crash data on our behalf.
