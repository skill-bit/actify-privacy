Privacy Policy for Actify

Effective Date: December 2, 2025

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for using Actify (“we,” “our,” or “the app”). Your privacy is extremely important to us, and we intentionally designed Actify to collect as little data as possible.

Actify is an offline, charades-style party game. You can use the app without creating an account or providing any personal information. Any data collection is optional and used strictly for improving app stability.

No Account Required

  • Actify does not require you to sign in, create an account, or provide personal details.
  • All gameplay takes place entirely offline.

Information We Collect

1. Crash and Diagnostic Data (Optional)

We collect diagnostic information only if you choose to keep crash reporting enabled. This helps us understand and fix app crashes.

What we may collect:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App version and build type
  • Crash stack traces and internal error logs
  • Game state at the time of crash (e.g., deck selected, score, timer settings)
  • A random, non-personal "instance ID" generated by Firebase Crashlytics to group crash reports. This identifier cannot identify you and resets if you reinstall the app.

2. Analytics Data (Automatically Collected)

We collect anonymous usage analytics to understand how you use Actify and improve the app experience.

What we collect:

  • App interactions (screens viewed, buttons tapped)
  • Game sessions (decks played, scores, game duration)
  • Settings preferences (sound, haptics enabled/disabled)
  • Device information (model, OS version, app version)
  • Install date and usage frequency
  • A random Firebase Analytics instance ID (automatically generated, cannot identify you personally)

How it's collected:

Using Firebase Analytics, provided by Google LLC. This data is aggregated and anonymized.

What analytics helps us do:

  • Understand which game decks are most popular
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Identify and fix user experience issues
  • Prioritize new features based on actual usage patterns

How it’s collected:

Using Firebase Crashlytics, a crash reporting service provided by Google LLC.

User control:

Crash reporting is enabled by default, but you may opt out at any time in the app:

Settings → Crash Reports → OFF

What we do not collect:

  • No names, emails, or phone numbers
  • No precise location
  • No contacts, photos, media, or files
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No browsing or app activity outside of Actify
  • No user-generated content sent to our servers (custom decks are stored locally on your device only)

How We Use Information

Crash, diagnostic, and analytics data are used exclusively to:

  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve performance and stability
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Prioritize feature improvements based on actual usage
  • Make the app more enjoyable and user-friendly

We do not:

  • Sell your data
  • Use data for advertising or marketing
  • Share data with third parties, except Google Firebase as our service provider
  • Personally identify you from analytics data

Data Storage and Security

Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)

Crash data is processed and stored by Google in accordance with their privacy policies and security standards.

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase Privacy & Security: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Retention:

Crash data is retained for up to 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted.

Security:

Google implements industry-standard safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Your Privacy Rights

Opt Out of Crash Reporting

You are always in control. To stop sending crash reports:

  1. Open Actify
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Turn Crash Reports to OFF

No diagnostic data will be collected once disabled.

Right to Deletion

Because we do not collect personal or account-based data, there is no personal profile to delete. Crash data is anonymized and automatically deleted after 90 days.

If you have concerns about your data, you can contact us at: actify.game@gmail.com

GDPR Information (EU Users)

If you reside in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we process
  • Rectify/Erase: Request deletion or correction (note: data is anonymized)
  • Object: Disable crash reporting at any time

We rely on legitimate interest for optional crash diagnostics, but you may opt out at any time by disabling crash reporting in the app settings.

CCPA Information (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what categories of data are collected
  • Opt out of data collection (by disabling crash reporting)
  • Request deletion (crash data is anonymized and time-limited)

We do not sell or share data for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)

Purpose: Crash reporting and diagnostics

Data Collected: Device info, crash logs, app version, game state at crash time, random instance ID

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

Purpose: Usage analytics and app improvement

Data Collected: App interactions, screen views, game sessions, device info, random instance ID

Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google acts as a data processor. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized, and is not used for advertising purposes.

Children’s Privacy

Actify is family-friendly and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

  • No accounts or personal data are required to use the app
  • Crash data is anonymized and does not identify individual users
  • Parents may disable crash reporting at any time in the app settings

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at actify.game@gmail.com.
